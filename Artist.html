AUDIBLE ARTIST

Coloring is a fun pastime for people of all ages, and Audible Artist is an online coloring book that allows the artist to channel their creativity in a cool and unique way: using just their voice. With Audible Artist, artists can choose from over 140 different colors and a wide variety of coloring pages c create their very own artwork and share it with friends, family, or to even start a collection for themselves. With voice recognition, Audible Artist makes coloring easy by having artists interact with the computer and tell it exactly what to color on a coloring page and what color it should be. Audible Artist and its novel voice feature is the perfect application for children, adults, and for those with or without disability.
